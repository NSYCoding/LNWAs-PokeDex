Brock

Brock is a Pokémon Trainer from Pewter City.

My Goals

Are to help Pokémon and their trainers become stronger. Improve my knowledge and skills as a Gym Leader.

Also to contribute to Pokémon breeding and healthcare.

My Pain Points

Challenges in tracking and analyzing the health and growth patterns of various Pokémon.

Need for a comprehensive resource to guide Pokémon breeding and training.

Limited access to in-depth information on Pokémon moves and their strategic applications.
